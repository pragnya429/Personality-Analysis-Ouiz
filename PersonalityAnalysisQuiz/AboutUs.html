Welcome to KnowYou!

Welcome to KnowYou, where self-discovery meets the power of the Enneagram! Our site is dedicated to helping you explore the depths of your personality and gain deeper insight into your unique strengths, challenges, and motivations.

Our Mission

At KnowYou, we believe that understanding your personality is a powerful tool for personal growth and meaningful relationships. Using the Enneagram, a centuries-old model that identifies nine distinct personality types, our quizz offers you the chance to uncover your core motivations, fears, and desires—helping you navigate life with greater self-awareness.

Who We Are

Created by a passionate psychology and Enneagram enthusiast, KnowYou is committed to offering quizz that is both scientifically grounded and highly engaging. We aim to provide you with an enriching experience that goes beyond surface-level personality tests.

What Sets Us Apart

Unlike many other personality quizzes, our focus is on the Enneagram system—an insightful tool for understanding the core of who you are. We’ve designed our quizzes to not only give you results but to help you understand how your personality type influences your behaviors, relationships, and goals in life.

Our Philosophy

We believe that by understanding our Enneagram type, we can unlock our true potential and foster deeper connections with others. No matter where you are on your journey of self-discovery, KnowYou is here to support you in embracing your authentic self.
